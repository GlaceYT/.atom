Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I open a new account?

You can open a new account by visiting any HDFC branch or through our online portal.

What documents are required for account opening?

You need to provide proof of identity, proof of address, and recent photographs.

How can I reset my internet banking password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Forgot Password' link on the internet banking login page.

What are the customer support contact details?

You can reach our customer support at 1800-123-456 or email support@hdfcbank.com.
